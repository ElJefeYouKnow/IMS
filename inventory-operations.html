Inventory Operations

Operations Control

  • Check-In: log arrivals or new on-the-fly purchases (requires name for new codes).
  • Pick (Check-Out): draws from available after reservations, auto-sets 5-day due.
  • Reserve: holds stock for a job; remove from available until released.
  • Return: allowed within 5 days of checkout; enforces outstanding limits.
